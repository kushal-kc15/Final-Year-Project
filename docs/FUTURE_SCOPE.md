# Future Scope

## Production Deployment

Deploy the backend and frontend to production infrastructure with PostgreSQL, persistent media storage, HTTPS, monitoring, and backups.

## Advanced Receipt AI

Improve receipt extraction accuracy with stronger document parsing, vendor detection, tax extraction, currency detection, and duplicate receipt checks.

## PDF Reports

Add downloadable PDF reports for monthly summaries, vendor reports, budget usage, and approval history.

## Audit Trail Improvements

Expand activity logs with before/after values, export history, actor IP/device metadata, and administrator review tools.

## Organization Billing and Subscriptions

Add subscription plans, usage limits, paid workspaces, billing history, and owner billing controls.

## Mobile App

Build a mobile app or PWA focused on quick expense submission, receipt capture, and approval notifications.

## Advanced Analytics

Add anomaly detection, recurring vendor insights, spending forecasts, budget recommendations, and category-level trends.

## Role Customization

Introduce custom permission sets beyond owner and staff, such as finance reviewer, auditor, or department manager.

## Integrations

Future integrations could include accounting tools, bank feeds, Slack or email alerts, and cloud receipt storage.
