# Project Overview

Vyapar Margadarshan is a business expense management platform built for small organizations, student project evaluation, and portfolio demonstration. The system gives each workspace a controlled place to record spending, collect receipts, review expenses, track budgets, and report on approved business costs.

## What It Does

The platform supports a complete expense lifecycle:

1. A workspace owner creates an organization.
2. Staff members are invited to the workspace.
3. Staff submit expenses with amount, category, vendor, date, notes, and optional receipt.
4. Owners approve or reject expenses.
5. Rejected expenses can be corrected and resubmitted.
6. Approved expenses feed budgets, reports, vendor analytics, dashboards, and CSV exports.

## Target Users

- Small business owners who need visibility into team spending.
- Staff members who need a simple way to submit expenses.
- Account or finance reviewers who need approved-only reports.
- Platform administrators who need a website manager console through Django Admin.

## Why It Is Useful

Expense tracking often becomes unreliable when receipts, approvals, and reports live in separate tools. Vyapar Margadarshan centralizes the process and adds workspace scoping, role permissions, approved-only analytics, and a clear audit trail.

## Business Problem Solved

The project reduces manual expense tracking work, improves accountability, separates pending expenses from approved spend, and helps owners make budget decisions using real approved data.
